City Planner Game

You are required to assign land in a development area into zones.

Each section in the 5x5 area may be zoned as either Residential ("R") or Commercial ("C"), with a maximum of 12 Residential ("R") tiles.

Part 1: Try to maximise the total score of the layout, which is the sum of the 5 row scores and 5 column scores. Each row/column score is determined by the number of "R"s and "C"s it contains, as given in the table.

Part 2: Now try to minimise the total score of the layout.

Hint: Both your solutions are optimal only when the max is 30 points higher than the min

#R #C Score
5 0 10
4 1 9
3 2 8
2 3 2
1 4 1
